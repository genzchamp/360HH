What is Forex?

Forex (Foreign Exchange) is the market where currencies are exchanged. You are always trading one currency against another.

Example: EUR/USD means Euro vs US Dollar.

Types of Markets

  • Forex: EURUSD, GBPUSD, USDJPY
  • Gold: XAUUSD
  • Crypto: BTCUSD, ETHUSD

Buy & Sell (Very Important)

Buy: You expect price to go up.
Sell: You expect price to go down.

Pips, Lots & Spread

Pip: Smallest price movement.
Lot: Trade size.
Spread: Difference between Buy & Sell price.
