Forex Basics

Understand trading the halal & simple way

What is Forex?

Forex (Foreign Exchange) is the market where currencies are traded. You are not gambling — you are exchanging one currency for another based on economic value.

Currency Pairs

Forex is traded in pairs. Example:

  • EUR/USD → Euro vs US Dollar
  • GBP/USD → British Pound vs US Dollar
  • XAU/USD → Gold vs US Dollar

Buy & Sell (Halal Concept)

Buy means you believe the first currency will gain value. Sell means you believe it will lose value.

This is similar to real trade — not betting. Avoid interest (swap/overnight fees) to keep it halal.

Pips & Price Movement

A pip is a small price movement. Profits and losses are calculated based on pips.

Leverage (Warning)

Leverage allows you to control larger trades with small capital. It is a tool, not free money.

Use it carefully. Over-leverage destroys accounts.
