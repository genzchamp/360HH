Forex Trading Sessions

Know when the market actually moves

Why Sessions Matter

The forex market is open 24 hours, but price does not move the same all day. Liquidity and volatility depend on trading sessions.

Main Forex Sessions

  • Asian Session — Tokyo
  • London Session — London
  • New York Session — New York

Asian Session (Tokyo)

Usually ranges and consolidates. Good for range traders, not ideal for beginners.

London Session

The most volatile session. Breakouts and strong moves happen here.

New York Session

Often continues or reverses London moves. News releases happen here.

Best Session for Beginners

Focus on London or London → New York overlap. Avoid overtrading multiple sessions.
